Motors:
    (Circuit board pins to motor board looking from the front)
 - RIGHT_PWM = Pin 9 -> ENA (Far left)
 - RIGHT_REV = Pin 4 -> IN1 (Left)
 - RIGHT_FWD = Pin 5 -> IN2 (Left middle)
 - LEFT_FWD = Pin 8 -> IN3 (Right middle)
 - LEFT_REV = Pin 7 -> IN4 (Right)
 - LEFT_PWM = Pin 11 -> ENB (Far right)
